Solver GPT - AI Chatbox Application: A Streamlit-based platform enabling users to upload and interact with multiple PDFs.

Text Segmentation and Embeddings: It segments uploaded text into chunks and converts them into embeddings using OpenAI or HuggingFace Embeddings, optimizing for efficient similarity searches.

ConversationalRetrievalChain: This feature integrates a pre-trained ChatOpenAI model with a FAISS vector store, providing users with responses relevant to their queries directly from the PDF content.

User Experience: Users can easily upload PDFs and receive contextualized responses from the application, enhancing their interaction with document content.
